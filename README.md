##  Project Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard is an interactive Power BI solution designed to analyze employee attrition and workforce trends. This dashboard helps HR professionals identify key factors influencing employee turnover and supports data-driven decision-making for employee retention strategies.

---

##  Business Problem

Employee attrition can significantly impact organizational performance and increase recruitment costs. This dashboard provides insights into:

- Employee Attrition Rate
- Demographic Trends
- Department-wise Attrition
- Salary Impact on Attrition
- Employee Tenure Analysis
- Job Role Analysis

##  Key Insights

### Employee Attrition

- Total Attrition: **237 Employees**
- Overall Attrition Rate: **16.1%**

### Age Analysis

- Highest attrition observed in employees aged **26-35 years**.
- Younger workforce shows a higher tendency to leave.

### Salary Analysis

- Majority of attrition occurs in employees earning **up to 5K salary**.
- Attrition decreases as salary increases.

### Job Role Analysis

- Laboratory Technicians have the highest attrition count.
- Sales Executives and Research Scientists follow closely.

### Education Analysis

- Employees from Life Sciences and Medical backgrounds contribute most to attrition.

---

## Business Recommendations

### Improve Employee Retention
Focus on employees within the first few years of employment.

### Salary Optimization
Review compensation structures for lower salary bands.

### Career Growth Programs
Develop learning and promotion opportunities for employees aged 26–35.

### Department-Level Monitoring
Regularly monitor departments and job roles with higher turnover rates.
